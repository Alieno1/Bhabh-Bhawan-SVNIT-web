Our Facilities

Hostel Bhabha Bhavan offers a comprehensive range of facilities designed to ensure a comfortable and enriching experience for its residents. Some of the key facilities provided include:

Conference Room

Facilitates academic and organizational activities.

Laboratory

Enables practical learning opportunities.

Laundry Room

Facility for washing and drying clothes.

Mess

Provides nutritious meals catering to diverse dietary needs.

Night Canteen

Offers snacks and refreshments during late hours.

Reading Room

A quiet space for focused study and research.

Sports Facilities

Promotes physical activity and camaraderie.

Store Room

Houses essential supplies for daily operations.

Student Rooms

Residential accommodations for residents.

Security

Ensures the safety and well-being of residents.

TV Room

Recreational area for relaxation and entertainment.

Warden's Office

Administrative hub for guidance and support.
